At CALL, we believe that high-quality nutrition and education are two of the most potent forces of positive change in society.

Our aim is to ensure:

  1. Everyone has access to food.
  2. Every child has the educational resources they need to fulfill their potential ( or can say “Lack of educational resources never come in the way of the hope of a brighter future”).

Your donations make this possible!

Key Activities

We identify and support:

  1. Underprivileged people with little to no income by providing them with one meal a day.
  2. Students from low-income backgrounds by providing them with scholarships to complete their education.

Our Approch:

In order to achieve our goal, we identify and employ local entrepreneurs who are in need of a sustainable livelihood to cook and distribute fresh and nutritious meals. These meals are then packaged in eco-friendly materials, and distributed amongst underprivileged individuals who are in need of food.

We also identify high-potential students who are at risk of dropping out from the educational system and provide them with scholarships and educational resources to complete their education, so that they can fulfill their complete potential.
