This is a home for the destitute and abandoned adults. New Life presently supports 70 aged people, who either have no immediate family members or have been abandoned by their kith and kin. At Saranagathi, they are provided food, shelter, clothing and medical aid, along with love, care and affection. They feel at home here and can spend the rest of their lives peacefully.
